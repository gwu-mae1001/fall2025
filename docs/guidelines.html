This workshop requires a combination of individual and group work entailing hands-on activities and frequent interactions with the instructor.


classroom by Chattapat from Noun Project

We ask that all attendees adhere to the following workshop guidelines and responsibilities while attending:

  • Be respectful: Limit the use of personal devices. Be aware that you are working in a group. Be mindful of your colleagues.
  • Be responsible: Keep an open mind to the course material presented. Help your team members.
  • Be a communicator: Observe, Ask questions and Try out the materials presented during the workshop. Communicate with your team effectively and politely.
  • Be a problem solver: Explore options to complete hands-on tasks. Make your own notes. Stay positive about the workshop outcomes.

Justice, Equity, Diversity, Inclusion (JEDI) statement

The instructor intends to students from all diverse backgrounds and perspectives by this workshop. The diversity that the attendees bring to this workshop is viewed as a resource, strength, and benefit. The workshop materials and activities presented are intended to be respectful of: age, race, ethnicity, country of origin, language, religion, spiritual practice, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, introversion/extroversion personality dimensions, and socioeconomic and mental/physical status.
